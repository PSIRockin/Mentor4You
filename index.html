Mentor4You

Who We Are

Mentor4You is a web/mobile web app that allows teens in middle/high school to be paired with mentors in their desired career field. Mentors and students would login differently, with the mentors signing up with their LinkedIn to establish credibility, and students will be able to search for them by field, distance, and position and contact them via Direct Message, Voice, or Video Call.

What We Do / Benefits

Our program is focused on the youth and helping them to strive for nothing but success. Every Child should be able to sign up and receive help from a mentor in their preferred career choice or a topic that they are struggling in.

  • Increased high school graduation rates
  • Lower high school dropout rates
  • Healthier relationships and lifestyle choices
  • Better attitude about school
  • Enhanced self-esteem and self-confidence
  • Improved behavior, both at home and at school
  • Stronger relationships with parents, teachers, and peers
  • Improved interpersonal skills
